          why rathinam

          A Built-in Business Incubation Centre

          Rathinam Group owns and operates its own Business Incubation Center. AIC RAISE backs for-profit, mission-driven founders and startups building tech to solve social & environmental problems. It creates a holistic ecosystem for social startups for maximizing profits while at the same time benefiting the society and the environment established under the prestigious Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) of NITI Aayog, Govt. of India. If you are a passionate, aspiring entrepreneur with a strong drive to change the society around you, AIC RAISE is a perfect business incubator that will nurture your dream of success.

          Educational Loan

          Educational Loan Assistance for Rathinam Group Students.

          The goal of Rathinam Group is to assist deserving/meritorious students in obtaining financial aid to further their study. To encourage students to pursue higher education despite financial constraints, most banks, whether state or federal government-owned, now offer attractive loan options to students.

          We believe that talent should be rewarded. Rathinam Group has established a separate loan aid unit to enable students to seek loans from banks with which the university has ties at the time of admission (online or offline).

          DOCUMENTS REQUIRED TO AVAIL EDUCATION LOAN

          From applicant Side

          1. KYC (Aadhaar Card/ Voter Card/ Driving License/ Passport).
          2. 2 Recent Passport Size Photographs.
          3. Mark Sheets/Passing Certificates of 10TH, 12TH/Diploma & Degree.
          4. PAN Card.

          From Rathinam Group Side

          1. Admission Offer Letter.
          2. Full Fee Structure of the Chosen Course.
          3. Prospectus of the University.
          4. Courses (Fee Payments Slips/ Registration Slip).
          5. Placement Record (previous year).
          6. UGC/AICTE Affiliation Letter/ Other Affiliation for Particular Courses.

          From Parents/ Guarantor Side

          1. KYC (Aadhaar Card/ Voter card/ Driving License/ Passport) .
          2. 2 Recent Passport Size Photographs.
          3. PAN Card.
          4. Last 6-month bank account statement.
          5. IT returns/ IT assessment order of previous 2 years/ Form no 16/16A/Salary Slip or Income Proof issued by Magistrate/Tehsildar .
          6. A brief statement of assets & liabilities of co-borrower/ guarantor.
