          why rathinam

          Rathinam, as a prestigious institution, is noted for its exceptional educational standards. The Rathinam Group is the first organization that combines education, IT development, and infrastructure development all under one roof.

          Built-in Business Incubation Centre

            Rathinam Group owns and operates its own Business Incubation Center. AIC RAISE backs for-profit, mission-driven founders and startups building tech to solve social & environmental problems. It creates a holistic ecosystem for social startups for maximizing profits while at the same time benefiting the society and the environment established under the prestigious Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) of NITI Aayog, Govt. of India. They aim to develop ideas that seem promising to the economy while at the same time deliver solutions for world problems. If you are a passionate, aspiring entrepreneur with a strong drive to change the society around you, AIC RAISE is a perfect business incubator that will nurture your dream of success.

                  Educational Loan

                  Educational Loan Assistance for Rathinam Group Students.

                  The goal of Rathinam Group is to assist deserving/meritorious students in obtaining financial aid to further their study. To encourage students to pursue higher education despite financial constraints, most banks, whether state or federal government-owned, now offer attractive loan options to students.

                  We believe that talent should be rewarded. Rathinam Group has established a separate loan aid unit to enable students to seek loans from banks with which the university has ties at the time of admission (online or offline).
