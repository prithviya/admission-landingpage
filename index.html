          why rathinam

          Rathinam, as a prestigious institution, is noted for its exceptional educational standards. The Rathinam Group is the first organization that combines education, IT development, and infrastructure development all under one roof.

          The Group provides aspiring students from all over the world a variety of conventional and modern courses. The campus is located on NH 209 in south Coimbatore and spans 70 acres. The organization, which has state-of-the-art infrastructure, selects its skilled and experienced teaching team with great care.

          It's where a slew of major MNC Companies recruit from a pool of well-educated, well-informed, and well-trained hands–on students.   Our innovative programme is a great way to stay motivated, stand out from the crowd, increase your value to future employers, and allow students to reach their maximum potential. Rathinam is committed to training tomorrow's leaders and providing students with a wide range of skills that employers are increasingly seeking.

            A Built-in Business Incubation Centre

            The Rathinam Group owns and operates its own Business Incubation Center.

            AIC RAISE backs for-profit, mission-driven founders and startups building tech to solve social & environmental problems. It creates a holistic ecosystem for social startups for maximizing profits while at the same time benefiting the society and the environment established under the prestigious Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) of NITI Aayog, Govt. of India. They aim to develop ideas that seem promising to the economy while at the same time deliver solutions for world problems. If you are a passionate, aspiring entrepreneur with a strong drive to change the society around you, AIC RAISE is a perfect business incubator that will nurture your dream of success.
